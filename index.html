Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services in Dublin

How long does a typical cleaning take?

Cleaning duration depends on the size of your space and service type. Typically:
• Standard cleaning: 2-6 hours
• Deep cleaning: 4-9 hours
• After renovation: 4-9 hours
We'll provide an exact time estimate during booking.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

No, we bring all necessary professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. We use eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets. If you have specific product preferences, just let us know!

Can I schedule regular recurring cleanings?

Absolutely! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleaning plans with discounted rates for regular customers. You'll get the same trusted cleaner each time, and we're flexible with scheduling changes.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It's your choice! Many clients provide us with keys or door codes for convenient access. All our staff are fully vetted and insured. We also offer secure key storage if you prefer.

What's included in the final price?

Everything is included: professional-grade supplies, equipment, trained cleaners. No hidden fees..

Are your cleaners insured and vetted?

Yes! All our cleaners undergo thorough background checks, are fully insured, and receive regular training. Your property and privacy are completely protected.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact us directly for any specific inquiries.
